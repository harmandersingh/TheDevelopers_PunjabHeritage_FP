Punjab Tour Packages

Punjab Tours is a major attraction of Punjab. A vibrant state, Punjab has a rich and long past. Witness to numerous reigns from the Aryans to the British, the state is dotted by royal palaces, forts, temples and shrines. Punjab is known as the land of five rivers. The state is famous for its history, culture and cuisine. Being a major tourist destination, there are a number of tour packages of Punjab. To enjoy your tour, it is sensible to book one of the many Punjab tour packages available. You can visit Punjab Tour Planner for some tour packages of Punjab.
